Ethos

Using black, white, and electric blue as an accent color can help to create a clean, modern, and sophisticated design that is relevant to the gaming industry and appropriate for a UX designer. The font choices of Libre Baskerville and Lato support the minimalistic design and professional feel.

Electric blue is a popular color choice for many video game interfaces and branding, so incorporating this color as an accent glow helps to establish a connection to the gaming industry. It can also communicate your familiarity with gaming design conventions and my ability to create designs that are relevant to this industry.
